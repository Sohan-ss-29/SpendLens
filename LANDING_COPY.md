## FAQ (5 Questions)

**Q1: Is this actually free?**
Yes. Completely free, no credit card, no trial period. We make money when you use Credex credits to capture savings — and that only happens if you choose to.

**Q2: How accurate is the pricing data?**
We source pricing from official vendor pricing pages and update it monthly. For API-based tools (OpenAI, Anthropic), we use public token pricing — actual usage-based costs vary. See `PRICING_DATA.md` for sources and dates.

**Q3: Do you store my data?**
We store your tool selections and savings figures (no personal data) to generate your shareable audit URL. Your email is only collected if you choose to save your results — and is never sold or shared.

**Q4: What tools do you support?**
Currently: Cursor, GitHub Copilot, ChatGPT Plus/Team, Claude Pro/Team, Gemini Advanced, Windsurf, OpenAI API, Anthropic API, and Google AI Studio. More tools added based on user demand.

**Q5: What is Credex and how does it relate?**
Credex is an AI procurement platform that negotiates bulk discounts with AI vendors and passes savings to startups. SpendLens is a free tool built by Credex to help founders understand their spend. There's no obligation to use Credex after using SpendLens.
